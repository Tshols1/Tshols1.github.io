PARGO LOCKERS

We are introducing our new locker system that will be found at various petrol stations:

  • It is a safe and secure
  • Easy to retrieve your parcel
  • No hussle
  • All you need is a collection PIN

How it works:

  1. Please your online order with one of our suppliers
  2. At check out choose one of our Pick Up Lockers
  3. Our driver will collect your parcel from the supplier and drop it off at your chosen locker
  4. Parcel will be delivered in 1-3 business days
  5. Once the parcel is delivered, a Unique Collection Pin will be sent to your email and mobile number
  6. Your parcel will be kept safely at our lockers for a period of 10 days

Our lockers are in ALL the 9 provinces;

  • Gauteng
  • Western Cape
  • KwaZulu Natal
  • Limpopo
  • North West
  • Eastern Cape
  • Northern Cape
  • Mpumalanga
  • Free State
